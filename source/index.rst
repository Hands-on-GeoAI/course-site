**Introduction to Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI)** -course introduces you to the foundational approaches and methods of machine learning and artificial intelligence applied to spatial data. After the course, you can identify appropriate machine learning algorithms for different geospatial tasks, and describe data needs and suitable training procedures for a given analysis process. You can critically discuss concepts such as spatially explicit AI, explainability, fairness, and bias.
The course has been developed collaboratively at the Department of Built Environment, Aalto University, Finland, and the materials are openly available for anyone interested.

.. important::
    Notice that the **course starts on Monday 16th March 2026**. Teaching sessions are held in Otakaari 4.

Learning objectives
-------------------

After completing this course, you should:

- understand the history, classification, and fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence
- understand how spatial information is integrated into AI (spatially explicit AI)
- understand machine learning basics (supervised and unsupervised learning, regression, classification)
- know the standard terminology, training procedures, and evaluation methods of ML models
- be able to train and evaluate a simple ML model using Python
- critically assess concepts in AI such as explainability, fairness, and bias

Prerequirements
---------------

Before taking this course, it is required to know the basics of Python programming as well as GIS.

**Python basics:** If you are new to Python, or would like to refresh your Python skills, we recommend to go through the Part 1 materials (Chapters 1-4) on the open online book,
`Introduction to Python for Geographic Data Analysis <https://pythongis.org/>`__ by Tenkanen, Heikinheimo & Whipp (2025). In case you want to earn credits (5 ECTS) while learning the basics of Python,
we recommend to take a course called Geo-Python at University of Helsinki which is available at `geo-python.github.io <http://geo-python.github.io/>`__.
You can `enroll to this course via Sisu <https://sisu.aalto.fi/student/courseunit/otm-745028e1-2a87-4eb1-abce-ee708626fcb6/brochure>`__ (i.e. if you are a student at Aalto University).

Course format
-------------

The majority of this course will be spent in front of a computer writing code with the Python language.
The course consists of 3-hour teaching sessions that integrate theoretical lectures and practical laboratories. The exercises will focus on applying the introduced machine learning methods to given geographical problems.

Most exercises in this course involve real world examples and data. For each exercise, you may be asked to submit the Python codes you have written, output figures and answers to related questions. You are encouraged to discuss and work together with other students while working on the weekly exercises.

.. admonition:: Aalto University students

    The course is part of the
    `Master's Programme of Geoinformatics at Aalto University <https://www.aalto.fi/en/study-options/masters-programme-in-geoinformatics>`__.
